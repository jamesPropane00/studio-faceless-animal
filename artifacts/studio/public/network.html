How to Join

Three Steps
to the Board.

No gatekeeping. No application process. Fill out the form, send your content, and your page goes live.

01

Pick Your Plan

Start at $0 with a free presence page — or go straight to Starter ($30), Pro ($55), or Premium ($105). All plans put you on the board. Paid plans unlock a custom domain, media sections, and enhanced design — setup and platform access bundled, no separate charge.

02

Submit Your Info

Fill out the intake form with your name, handle, bio, links, and a profile image. For paid plans with media sections, we'll follow up for anything else we need. We handle the build — you handle the content.

03

Your Page Goes Live

Within 3–5 business days your page is built, published, and listed on the Creator Board. You get your URL — facelessanimalstudios.com/yourname — and you're inside the ecosystem from day one.
