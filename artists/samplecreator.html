About

Three Years Shooting
What Other People Walk Past.

KOLD Visual is the street photography and digital art project of a Providence-based visual artist who started shooting on a second-hand Canon in 2022 and hasn't put the camera down since. The work is dark, intentional, and shot mostly at night — alleys, intersections, storefronts, faces that don't know they're being watched.

The visual identity is high-contrast monochrome with occasional color-graded series. No filters. No presets. Everything processed in-house with a consistent look that builds across series. The goal is work that feels like a record — something that shows the city at a specific moment in time.

Currently building out a print series and accepting commission work for editorial, event, and brand photography. All inquiries through email or DM.
