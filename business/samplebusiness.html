About

Five Years.
One Standard.

Blackout Cuts opened in 2021 on Westminster Street in Providence's Federal Hill neighborhood. We run a tight shop — two chairs, one standard. Every cut leaves the same way: clean fade, sharp lines, no complaints.

We do high, mid, and low fades tailored to your head shape. Beard shaping from full sculpts to light edge work. Hot towel finishes. Kids cuts for 12 and under. We don't rush it and we don't cut corners. If your lineup isn't straight, it isn't done.

Walk-ins are welcome Tuesday through Saturday. Call ahead on Saturdays — those seats go fast. No appointment required, but we keep a text waitlist if you want to hold your spot before you walk in.
