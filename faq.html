Signal

What Signal
Actually Means.

What is Signal on this platform?

Signal is the live social layer of Faceless Animal Studios. It connects identity pages, posting, radio, directory discovery, and private communication in one system. It is designed for creators and communities that want presence without algorithm pressure.

How is this different from a normal social network?

Signal is not built around follower games, ad-driven engagement loops, or real-name identity. You operate through platform-native identity (handle + Signal ID), publish on your terms, and connect through direct communication and creator pages.

How do radio, directory, pages, and feed connect?

Radio is the live listening layer. Directory is discovery. Creator pages are persistent identity hubs. The feed is live activity. DMs/calls are direct communication. Signal is the system that binds all of those into one user experience.

Identity & Recovery

Anonymous-First
Access.

Do I need email to create an account?

No. Account identity is based on your handle, Signal ID, password, and private recovery code. Email is not required for signup, login, recovery, messaging, or admin access in the active app flow.

What is the recovery code and why does it matter?

Recovery code is your ownership key. It is generated and stored as part of account setup. Save it immediately. If both password and recovery code are lost, the account cannot be restored.

Can support manually recover my account without my code?

No. The policy is intentionally strict to protect anonymous ownership and prevent social-engineering takeovers. No recovery code means no restoration path.

Communication

Messages & Calls
Rules.

Are DMs and calls paid features?

No. DMs and calls are core platform communication features and are free by default.

What can be gated by premium plans?

Storage-heavy features (file uploads, attachment storage, and premium creator tooling) can be gated by plan. Communication access can be restricted by moderation controls when safety policy requires it.

Where does voice changer fit?

Voice changing is treated as part of the communication stack, not a cosmetic extra. It belongs in the same layer as messaging and calls and is part of the product direction for anonymous expression.

Posting & Feed

How Feed Activity
Works.

What can I post?

Signal supports text updates, announcements, status updates, link shares, image references, audio/music references, and event-style posts. Posting tools are integrated into the dashboard feed composer.

Will my post show instantly in public?

Your post shows in your account history immediately. Public stream visibility follows moderation state and visibility rules.

Why does the feed use moderation states?

Moderation states keep the platform alive without becoming spam-heavy. This allows open posting while preserving quality and safety across public feed surfaces.

Moderation

Enforcement &
Account Safety.

What actions can moderators/admins take?

Moderation controls include warnings, account limits, suspensions, bans, role changes, and soft-delete workflows. Actions depend on role and policy scope.

What happens if an account is suspended?

Suspended accounts can be restricted from posting, publishing, communication, or premium tools depending on enforcement level and duration.

How is file sharing different from messaging?

Messaging is core communication. File-sharing is storage-heavy and may be plan-gated or moderated more aggressively. This keeps communication open while controlling abuse and infrastructure cost.

What do I get with the free tier?

The Free Presence plan is a real hosted identity page — not a trial, not a reduced preview. Here's exactly what's included:

Yes. $0 forever. No credit card required, no trial period, no auto-upgrade, no expiration date. The "Powered by Faceless Animal" badge stays on the page — that's the only trade-off. Your URL and content are yours as long as the platform runs.

Can I upgrade from Free to a paid plan later?

Yes. Upgrading is intentionally seamless. Your bio, links, photo, and URL all carry over — nothing gets wiped. Creator Pages

Building Your
Presence.

How long does setup take?

Most pages are live within 3–5 business days after we have everything we need from you — bio, links, photo, and any additional content for your plan level. The clock starts once we have your complete assets, not from the day you submit the form.

  • Free & Starter: 3–5 business days
  • Pro: 5–7 business days (additional sections)
  • Premium: Up to 2 weeks (fully custom, revisions included)

We'll confirm your timeline when we reply to your intake.

Do I need my own domain?

No. Free and Starter pages are hosted at facelessanimalstudios.com/yourname — no domain purchase needed. If you want your own URL (like yourname.com or yourband.com), the Pro and Premium plans include custom domain connection.

Domain registration is handled by you through any registrar (Namecheap, Google Domains, etc.) — you own it. We handle the connection and configuration. Domain costs are not collected by us.
What do I need to provide to get my page built?

The basics are the same for every plan:

  • Your display name and preferred page handle
  • Short bio (1–3 sentences)
  • Profile photo (square, minimum 500×500px)
  • Links to your platforms (Spotify, YouTube, Instagram, etc.)

Higher-tier plans may need additional content — a longer bio, social media handles, press photos, or specific sections. We'll tell you exactly what's needed when we confirm your plan.

What makes this different from Linktree or a standard bio link?

Linktree is a widget. These are full custom-built HTML pages with real design, real identity, and room to grow into a complete creative presence.

  • Your page lives on a platform with cultural credibility — not a generic bio link service
  • Dark, premium, underground aesthetic — not startup-clean templates
  • Part of an active creator network, not an isolated link dump
  • Upgradeable into full artist/business/game dev pages with real sections
  • Built by a human who understands the culture, not generated by an AI template builder
Can I update my page after it goes live?

Yes. Minor updates — link changes, bio edits, swapping a photo — are included. Larger structural changes or full redesigns may be quoted separately based on scope. Reach out via email with what you need and we'll give you a straight answer on what it involves.
