Common Questions

Before You
Decide.

Is the free tier actually free forever?

Yes. Free Presence is $0 forever. No trial period, no credit card, no expiration. The only difference from paid plans is the small "Powered by Faceless Animal Studios" badge at the bottom of your page. Everything else — the URL, the platform listing, the mobile-first layout — is real.

What's the $5/month for?

Platform access keeps your paid page active and listed in the Creator Network. It covers hosting, platform updates, and your spot on the platform roster. Free pages don't require it — the $5/month applies to Starter, Pro, and Premium plans only.

Are these subscriptions or one-time fees?

All paid plans — Starter ($30), Pro ($55), and Premium ($105) — are all-in via a single Stripe payment. Setup and platform access are bundled, nothing separate. Free is $0 forever, no monthly charge. Domain registration is handled through your own registrar and is not billed by Faceless Animal Studios.

Can I add a custom domain to my page later?

Yes. Custom domain connection is included with Pro and Premium plans. If you start at Free or Starter and want to add a domain later, upgrading to Pro handles the connection. You register the domain through your own registrar — we handle pointing it to your page.

Can I upgrade from Free to a paid plan without losing anything?

Your URL stays the same. Your bio, links, and profile carry forward. Upgrading adds features — it doesn't restart your page. Just reach out and tell us which tier you want to move to.

What do I need to send you to get started?

Fill out the intake form on the Get Started page. You'll share your name, handle, bio, links, and a profile image. For paid plans with media sections (Pro, Premium), we'll follow up for any additional assets. Most pages are live within 3–5 business days. Premium custom builds may take up to 2 weeks depending on scope.

What's the difference between Pro and Premium?

Pro gives you a strong creator page with a custom domain, music player, video section, press bio, and contact form — built on the platform's design system. Premium is a fully custom build: multi-section layout, deeper branding work, store or product integration, and revisions. If you need something that couldn't be templated, Premium is the path.
