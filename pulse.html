Audio Reactive App

Faceless Pulse.

Faceless Pulse is the music visualizer app inside the platform — built for radio, streams, edits, background motion, DJ sessions, branded loops, and pure sound-reactive energy. Load audio, switch modes, go fullscreen, and turn sound into movement.

What it is A branded visualizer tool for music, radio, content, and live visual energy inside the Faceless system.
What it does Reacts to your audio with 20 animated visual modes and a selector so you can lock the exact look you want.
Why it matters It gives you your own visual engine for lives, clips, edits, and platform media without relying on outside tools.
Where it fits Radio, TV, Stages, DJ sessions, stream overlays, promo content, and future creator tools.
