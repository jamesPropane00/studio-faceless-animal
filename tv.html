Faceless TV Upload Code

Faceless TV is an open video floor for creators. If it is yours and it is legal, it has a place here.

01 No corporate lanes

Music videos, shows, edits, podcasts, films, archives, experiments, and off-format drops can all live on Faceless TV.

02 Creator-owned only

Post your own work, your own channel material, or content you have permission to upload.

03 Illegal gets handled

Illegal content is removed. IP addresses and account data may be reported, and prosecution may be pursued where the law applies.

04 Everything else goes

Faceless TV is built to feel like a video platform without the corporate babysitting. Keep it legal. Keep it yours. Upload different.
